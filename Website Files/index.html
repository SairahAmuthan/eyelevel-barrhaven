Welcome to Eye Level Barrhaven

Eye Level is a supplemental education company that has learning centers located all over the world. Eye Level Barrhaven is the center located in Ottawa, Canada that provides Math and English services for kids from all over the city. Here, we allow your child to be the key to his or her academic success. Students will set their goals and master the step-by-step curriculum catered to their strengths and needs.



Exciting Changes

In addition to our thorough Math and English programs for students JK to Highschool, we have also added a Standard Level (equivalent to AP) and Higher Level Math program that applies to students in the International Baccalaureate Program. We have also begun to provide guidance for students thinking of applying to Gifted and IB. This includes ensuring students know the concepts featured in the CAT and CAT4 tests.
