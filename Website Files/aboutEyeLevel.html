What is Eye Level

Eye Level is a supplemental education company that promotes critical thinkers through self-directed learning. Eye Level is established in 20 countries and has received over 2 million enrollments over the years. Eye Level is based on an educational philosophy where students learn at their own pace. Eye Level empowers students to take ownership of their learning.
